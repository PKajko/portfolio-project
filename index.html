About Me

Hello, my name is Philip Kajko. I am currently learning web development through Codecademy's Full-Stack Web Development course.

I have been working as a fabricator and welder since 2014 but decided it wasn't what I would like to do for the forseeable future. I always had an interest in computers so decided to try to make the change to coding. I found Codecademy and completed the Choosing A Career In Tech course. Afterwhich I decided to persue a career in web development.

So far I have learned a lot about HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git Bash, Git Hub, and more. I am excited to continue learning and improving my skills in web development.

In my spare time I enjoy playing video games, streaming on Twitch, watching anime and making content for social media.
